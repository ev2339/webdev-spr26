Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • milk
  • butter

instructions

  1. step 1. mix eggs, flour, and milk in a bowl with a whisk and set stove on medium.
  2. step 2. put butter on stove. allow to simmer and then pour egg/flour/milk mixture onto pan. flip every 1-2 min.
  3. step 3. once pancakes are solid and golden brown, take off pan and enjoy with some toppings!
