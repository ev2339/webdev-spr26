~ vanilla cake ~

~ ingredients ~

flour

sugar

vanilla extract

butter

eggs

~ instructions ~

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl, making sure to sift the flour.

Step 3: Mix the wet ingredients together. Melt the butter in the microwave in increments of 30 seconds, and add to eggs once fully melted.

Step 4: Pour the cake batter into a greased pan and let it bake for 30-35 minutes. Check if fully baked by inserting a toothpick into the center of the cake; if the toothpick comes out dry, the cake is done!

Step 5: Let the cake cool down in the pan for at least 15 minutes.

Step 6: Frost the cake with whichever frosting and decorations you like! For this recipe, I recommend vanilla frosting.

Step 7: Enjoy!
