ABOUT ME!

Hello, I'm Esha, a cake baker and web developer! Made this website to showcase some of my best-selling cakes. You can find these cakes packaged in retail stores across NYC, and also at occasional pop up events! These recipes are all extremely beginner-friendly, quick, and delicous to eat. If you're a beginner baker looking for some to-go cake recipes, this is the website for you!

I started baking when I was in middle school, and have been doing it ever since. I these are cake recipes that I have perfected over all of these years. I hope to add more as I continue to bake and experiment with different flavors! I chose these three staple flavors because along with being the most popular ones I sell, I wanted this website to have something for everyone. Even if these recipes are too simple for you, feel free to add your own twist and just use these as a base! Thank you, and I hope you enjoy what this website has to offer.
